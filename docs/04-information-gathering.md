# Information Gathering

## Purpose

We gathered information to understand the current attendance and leave management process and to identify user requirements.

---

## Methods Used

### Internet Research

Studied existing attendance management systems and HR software.

### Observation

Observed manual attendance recording in organisations.

### Literature Review

Reviewed books and online resources related to Human Resource Management Systems.

### Existing Systems

Analyzed features provided by popular attendance management software.

---

## Functional Information Collected

- Employee Registration
- Attendance Recording
- Leave Request
- Leave Approval
- Attendance Reports
- Employee Dashboard

---

## Non-functional Information

- Security
- Performance
- Scalability
- Availability
- Reliability

---

## Findings

The study indicates that organisations require:

- Automated attendance
- Online leave application
- Quick approval process
- Accurate reporting
- Secure employee records
