# Interviews

## Objective

The interview aimed to understand the challenges faced by HR personnel and employees in managing attendance and leave.

---

## Interview Participant

| Name | Position |
|------|----------|
| HR Officer | Human Resource Department |

---

## Interview Questions

1. How is attendance currently managed?
2. How are leave requests submitted?
3. What problems occur frequently?
4. How long does leave approval take?
5. What improvements are required?

---

## Interview Responses

### Attendance

Attendance is maintained manually using spreadsheets.

### Leave Requests

Employees submit paper forms.

### Problems

- Lost records
- Delayed approval
- Human errors
- Difficulty generating reports

### Suggested Improvements

- Online attendance
- Digital leave requests
- Automatic reports
- Notification system

---

## Conclusion

The interview confirmed the need for an automated attendance and leave management system.
