# Stakeholder Analysis

## Introduction

Stakeholders are individuals or groups who interact with or benefit from the Employee Leave & Attendance Management System.

---

## Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibilities | Expectations |
|-------------|-----------------|--------------|
| Administrator | Manage system users and settings | Secure and reliable system |
| HR Manager | Approve leave, manage employees | Fast approval process |
| Employee | Mark attendance, request leave | Easy-to-use interface |

---

## Secondary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibilities |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Organization Management | Monitor workforce performance |
| IT Support | Maintain system availability |

---

## Stakeholder Needs

### Administrator

- Employee management
- Attendance monitoring
- Report generation

### HR Manager

- Leave approval
- Attendance reports
- Employee information

### Employee

- Attendance marking
- Leave application
- Leave status tracking

---

## Stakeholder Communication

| Stakeholder | Communication Method |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Administrator | Dashboard |
| HR Manager | Dashboard & Email |
| Employee | Dashboard & Notifications |
